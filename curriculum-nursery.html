Nursery Curriculum – Sunbeam Nursery

At Sunbeam Nursery, we believe that every child deserves a loving, nurturing environment to learn and grow. Our curriculum is designed to foster creativity, develop fine and gross motor skills, and encourage basic language and numeracy.

  • 🎨 Art and Craft activities to boost imagination
  • 📘 Storytelling and Rhymes for language development
  • 🧩 Puzzle solving and blocks for logical thinking
  • 🎵 Music and movement sessions for rhythm and motor skills
  • 🌱 Outdoor play for physical development and social interaction
