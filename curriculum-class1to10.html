Karnataka State Board Curriculum – Class I to X

Our school follows the Karnataka State Board (KSEEB) curriculum from Class I to X, ensuring holistic education that builds strong academic foundations. The curriculum includes:

  • 📖 Languages: English, Kannada, and Hindi
  • 🔬 Science and Environmental Studies
  • 📚 Mathematics with conceptual understanding
  • 🌍 Social Studies – History, Geography, Civics
  • 💻 Computer Education and Coding Basics
  • 🎭 Physical Education, Yoga, and Arts

The curriculum is regularly updated by the Department of State Educational Research and Training (DSERT) to meet modern academic standards and prepare students for the SSLC examination.
