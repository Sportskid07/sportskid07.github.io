School Culture & Traditions

Our school proudly upholds a rich culture built on values, inclusivity, and unity. Every tradition nurtures discipline, respect, and belonging in students.

Morning Assembly

Each day begins with a morning prayer, national anthem, thought of the day, news headlines, and value-based stories, promoting spiritual and intellectual growth.

Festivals & Celebrations

Major national and cultural festivals like Independence Day, Republic Day, Gandhi Jayanti, Diwali, and Christmas are celebrated with joy, performances, and decorations, nurturing unity in diversity.

Traditional Dress & Etiquette

Wednesdays and special events are designated for students to wear traditional attire, honoring Indian culture and modest dressing. Students are also taught respectful greetings like “Namaste.”

Cultural Events

The school conducts annual cultural programs, dance, drama, and music events to showcase student talents and preserve heritage arts.

Respect for Elders & Teachers

Students are encouraged to bow or greet teachers, touch books with reverence, and avoid stepping over learning materials, instilling humility and respect.
