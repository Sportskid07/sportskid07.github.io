Admissions

Admissions are open for the academic year 2025–26. Mahila Vidhyalaya Mandal's English Medium School welcomes applications from students for Pre-Primary to High School levels.

Admission Process

  1. Collect the application form from the school office or download it from our website.
  2. Submit the duly filled form with necessary documents.
  3. Attend the interaction session (for Pre-Primary) or entrance assessment (for Grades 1 and above).
  4. Admission confirmation will be notified by phone/email.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Nursery: Minimum age 3 years as on June 1st, 2025
  • LKG: Minimum age 4 years
  • UKG: Minimum age 5 years
  • Grade 1 and above: Based on performance in entrance test and availability of seats

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Aadhar card (student and parent)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Previous year’s report card (for Grade 1 and above)
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
